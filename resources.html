AI Fundamentals

Recommended Courses

AI for Everyone by Andrew Ng (Coursera)
Perfect starter course for non-technical understanding of AI. ~4 weeks of content.
Prompt Engineering for Developers by DeepLearning.AI
Learn to craft better AI prompts. Practical and immediately applicable.
Introduction to AI by IBM (Coursera/EdX)
Broader AI overview with real-world applications.

Learning Strategy

Don't try to learn everything at once! Here's a focused learning path:

  1. Week 1: Pick one AI introductory resource (AI for Everyone)
  2. Week 2-3: JavaScript basics from MDN or FreeCodeCamp
  3. Week 4-6: Pick one testing framework (Playwright or Cypress)
  4. Week 7+: Build your first project while learning

Key Principle: Learn by building, not by watching. Start projects early!
